A brick is a type of construction material used to build walls, pavements and other elements in masonry construction. Properly, the term brick denotes a unit primarily composed of clay, but is now also used informally to denote units made of other materials or other chemically cured construction blocks

Into different types based on their composition, method of manufacture, shape, size, color and durability. Some of the common types of bricks are

Clay bricks: These are made of clay and fired in kilns. They are the most traditional and widely used type of bricks. They can be further divided into categories such as wire-cut, extruded, molded, handmade, etc

Fly ash bricks: These are made of fly ash, cement and water. They are eco-friendly and cost-effective alternatives to clay bricks. They have high compressive strength and low water absorption
